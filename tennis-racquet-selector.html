Understanding the Specs

Head Size:

Larger heads (105-115 sq in) = more power and forgiveness. Smaller heads (95-98 sq in) = more control and precision.

Weight:

Lighter racquets (9-10 oz) = easier to swing, less power. Heavier racquets (11-12+ oz) = more power and stability, harder to maneuver.

Balance:

Head-heavy = more power from baseline. Head-light = better maneuverability and control at net.

String Pattern:

16x19 = more spin and power. 18x20 = more control and durability.

Swing Weight:

Lower (280-310) = easy to swing, less stable. Higher (320-340+) = more stable, requires good technique.
