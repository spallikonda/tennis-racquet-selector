Understanding Specs

Head Size:

Larger (105-115) = power & forgiveness. Smaller (95-98) = control & precision.

Weight:

Lighter (9-10oz) = easy to swing. Heavier (11-12oz+) = more power & stability.

Balance:

Head-heavy = baseline power. Head-light = net maneuverability.

String Pattern:

16x19 = spin & power. 18x20 = control & durability.
